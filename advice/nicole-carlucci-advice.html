Deciphering Mindset

Learning to code is like learning a completely new language, different from your native language and with a completely different system. Every obstacle is an opportunity and every aspect you don't understand is a challenge.

Only when you get into the mechanics of the language, into the deciphering mindset, you can really understand it.
